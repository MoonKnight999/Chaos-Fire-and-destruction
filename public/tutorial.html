Chaos: Fire and Destruction

The world has been attacked by a mysterious fire spreading monster. Defeat the monster or else....

  • Goal for monster: Burn all trees till there are no trees left. Hold [Spacebar] for fire.
  • Goal for planter: Make the monster collide with [Druids] and die OR plant 10 trees.
  • Controls for monster: [Left], [Right], [Up], or [Down] arrow to move.
  • Controls for planter: Click to create saplings. Saplings grow into trees and trees summon Druids.
  • Tips for monster: Move towards areas clustred with plants.
  • Tips for planter: Dont plant the saplings close so as to not get all of them burnt together.

Worst of luck! Have fun!
